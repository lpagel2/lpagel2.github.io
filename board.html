Board of Directors

The day to day operations of the PF HOA are run by an all-volunteer Board
of Directors, along with help from many other committee volunteers. The
Board establishes the annual dues assessment and is responsible for using
the money assessed to maintain and improve HOA property and offer programs
which are of value to Prairie Fields residents, thereby increasing the
value of the neighborhood.
Welcome new board members Brian Kesler and Kyle Kirchhoefer and returning board
members Greg Sopa, and Tim Yardley.
For more information, please email board@prairiefieldshoa.com
